# Playful Nonsense Patterns

## Fake-serious frameworks

- Three laws of [ordinary thing]
- A five-stage model of [minor inconvenience]
- The hidden economics of [daily habit]
- A mock research abstract about [silly topic]
- A fake operational manual for [non-serious scenario]

## Useful openings

- "下面是一本正经的玩笑版："
- "以下内容纯属胡说八道，但会努力胡说得有章法："
- "Mock-serious explanation, purely for fun:"
- "In a completely fictional research tradition..."

## Useful endings

- "当然，现实版解释就朴素多了。"
- "以上属于正经外壳包裹的离谱内容。"
- "If you want, I can now give the boring real answer."

## Avoid

- Real citations.
- Real institution names unless clearly parody-safe.
- Fake health, legal, finance, safety, or political claims.
- Anything that could be screenshotted out of context as factual.
